About Us

The Wisconsin family story goes along time ago, in 1899 Arthur Wisconsin wanted to create his own Whiskey, Arthur dreamed about this since he was kid, why because he saw how his father prepared the whiskey and he wanted to tried, so he did and for the first time, he discover that he had a talent to create and refine Whiskey, in that same year, with help of some friends, he started selling Whiskey during the prohibitions times, the word of the people travelled along the world and when he wasnt expected everyone came to taste his delicious Whiskey and that how Wisconsin Whiskey Shop was created.
